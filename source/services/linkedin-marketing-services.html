Why LinkedIn for B2B

The only social platform where business buyers actually engage

LinkedIn is the only social platform where decision-makers genuinely engage with business content. The reach is lower than Instagram or Facebook, but the audience quality is incomparable for B2B.

The catch: LinkedIn requires a different content approach. Long-form posts. Founder-led voice. Industry-specific insight. No memes, no GIFs, no sales-y noise. Our LinkedIn programmes are built around this reality.

  • Founder/exec thought leadership programmes
  • Long-form post strategy and ghostwriting
  • LinkedIn Ads (sponsored, message, ABM)
  • Account-based marketing alignment with sales
  • Employee advocacy programmes
  • CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce)

Frequently asked questions

Common questions about this service

Do you ghostwrite LinkedIn content for executives?
Yes — this is a core service. Discovery interviews to capture voice, content drafted in your style, you approve before posting. Most clients can barely tell the difference after three months.
Will LinkedIn Ads work for our business?
If you sell to other businesses with deal sizes over $5,000, almost certainly yes. LinkedIn Ads are expensive per click but high-quality. Below that deal size, the math is harder.
How quickly do LinkedIn results show?
Founder thought leadership: visible engagement growth in 30–60 days, pipeline impact in 4–6 months. LinkedIn Ads: lead flow within 30 days of proper setup.
Can you do LinkedIn outreach without being spammy?
Yes. Strategic, well-researched outreach to qualified prospects with personalized messages. No mass-blast, no automation tools that violate LinkedIn ToS, no embarrassing your brand.
